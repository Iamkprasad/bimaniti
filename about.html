BimaNiti tracks India's insurance sector — IRDAI regulatory developments, insurer performance, and market structure. Coverage spans life, general, health, and motor insurance, with a focus on what policy changes mean in practice rather than in theory. Every circular, result, and ruling is followed so that analysis stays grounded in what the regulator and the market actually did.

The approach is research-led and plain-language, with sources kept inline so nothing is asserted without a trail back to the original document. This is not a financial-advice platform — it is a curated reading resource for insurance professionals, practitioners, and informed policyholders who want to understand the sector, not just the headlines.
